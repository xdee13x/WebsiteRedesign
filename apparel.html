Apparel & Lettering

There are several methods of garment lettering; embroidery, screen printing, tackle twill (fabric letters) and heat-pressed vinyl letters. Please don't hesitate to consult with us in choosing the best method for your needs.

We embroider custom letters and designs on many different types of garments, in any quantity. The embroidery charge depends generally on the design size (stitch count), quantity and apparel type. We have 10 standard embroidery fonts that we can use without set-up. Logos and special fonts need to be digitized (set-up) before they can be embroidered.

Ordering Apparel for Lettering

You can bring in your own garments for embroidery. For a group order, however, you will find that ordering garments and lettering together with us saves you time and money, because we are a dealer for most major brands.
